A little about Marcella

The name is Marcella Telles

I am a First year college student attending Cleveland Institute of the Art. I am majoring in Industrial Design, but I am interested in all forms of art and design. It is something that has always been with me. I was born and raised in Miami, a hotspot for diversity. Living there allowed me to be exposed to graffiti, art exhibitions, art museums, and Art Basel. I am also a first generation american, the rest of my family comes from Brazil. I was constantly surrounded by different cultures. Growing up, there was only one person in my family that was an artist, and that was my grandma. She would sew and make carpets all day long. We would frequently doodle together and she was the one that first got me interested in drawing.
